# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data Utvekslingsportalen collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Utvekslingsportalen is a student-driven, non-commercial project and is **not an official NTNU service**.

## 2. Data we collect

### 2.1 User accounts and authentication

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

- Email address
- Account identifier provided by the authentication method

Authentication is handled using **Firebase Authentication**, including:

- Email and password login
- Google sign-in

We do **not** access your Google password or private Google account data.

### 2.2 User-submitted content

If you submit an exchange experience, we may store:

- University, country, year/semester
- Study program and specialization
- Course information (course codes, names, ECTS, replaced NTNU courses)
- Comments you choose to provide

Do **not** submit sensitive personal data or unnecessary personal information.

### 2.3 Analytics

Utvekslingsportalen uses **Google Analytics (GA4)** in production to understand how the platform is used and to improve functionality.

Analytics data is:

- Collected in aggregated form
- Used for usage statistics (e.g. page views, navigation patterns)
- Not used for advertising or profiling

## 3. How we use data

Collected data is used to:

- Authenticate users
- Display exchange experiences to other students
- Improve the platform and user experience
- Maintain security and prevent misuse

## 4. Storage and processing

Data is stored using **Firebase** on Google Cloud infrastructure. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored data.

## 5. Data sharing

- Personal data is **not sold**
- Data is **not shared for marketing or advertising**
- Analytics data is processed by Google under their standard terms

## 6. Data retention

Account data and submitted content is stored as long as necessary to operate the platform, or until deletion is requested.

## 7. Your rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to your data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account or submitted content
